East Bay Young Democrats

Political Director -
Jared McCreary

politics@cydprogressives.org

Sacramento County Young Democrats

Jared McCreary originally hails from Riverside, CA, where he attended UC Riverside and was a founding board member of the Riverside County Young Democrats. He first realized himself a progressive and Berniecrat in 2010 while interning on his first campaign. Since then he was worked on over a dozen races across the State and beyond, and now resides in Sacramento where he works as a policy advocate for the elderly and disabled. He also serves as VP of training for Generation Change, working to get millenials elected across the State of California. He now calls the Sacramento County Young Democrats his home chapter.

Cal Berkeley Democrats

Fundraising Director -
Liah Burnley

fundraising@cydprogressives.org

Liah is the Policy Advocate for Californians for Safety and Justice, a non-profit organization seeking to end wasteful prison and justice system spending, and the Racial and Social Justice Committee Chair of Organize Win Legislate, Sacramento. Liah is dedicated to organizing a wide base of young democrats that will advocate for our core progressive values.

Central Regional Director -

Southern Regional Director -
Gabriel Montoya

south.region@cydprogressives.org

CSULB College Democrats
Long Beach Young Democrats

Gabriel has worked extensively with the California Clean Money Campaign in gathering support and lobbying for the recently CA Disclose Act. Gabriel's passion for campaign finance reform and getting money out of politics is matched by his strong dedication towards forming connections and making sure all individuals know their voices matter in local affairs. As Southern Regional Director, Gabriel's main goal is to connect as many passionate individuals together in order to make our progressive voice stronger than ever before!
